Our Story

About Raja Ampat Dive Experience

We are a team of passionate ocean lovers dedicated to sharing the magic of the world's most biodiverse marine sanctuary with you.

An Adventure of a Lifetime.

At Raja Ampat Dive Experience, we take pride in delivering more than just a dive. We offer a gateway into an underwater paradise that few get to witness.

Located in the heart of the Coral Triangle, our dive center was founded on a deep respect for marine life and a commitment to safe, sustainable diving. Whether you are a beginner looking to take your first breath underwater or an experienced photographer hunting for macro critters, our local expertise ensures you are in the right place at the right time.
